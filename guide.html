Hosting Guide

The Global Day of Coderetreat has become a major worldwide software development event. As an organizer or facilitator of a coderetreat, you will help thousands of developers to improve their software development skills (which, in turn, will help to make the world a better place).

Below you will find important information about the Global Day of Coderetreat: everything you need in order to register, promote, and organize a great event. We regularly add new content so be sure to check back often!

Collaborate

The following resources will help you communicate and collaborate with other hosts and facilitators:
Discussion mailing list Discussion list for GDCR hosts and facilitators (potentially high traffic as we get closer to the event)
Annoucements mailing list Where important GDCR-related announcements are published (low traffic)
#gdcr Slack channel Get an invite and join #gdcr, the official GDCR Slack channel hosted on the Software Craftsmanship Slack "Team". Feel free to ask questions on the channel. You can also use the channel to coordinate video chats.
#gdcr16 Twitter hashtag for GDCR16. Watch this hashtag for exciting news around the world related to GDCR16!
Coderetreat Live The application we will use to show the world the current status of each coderetreat

Organize

The following resources will help you to organize and run your event:
Hosting FAQ Contains answers to common questions about hosting a Coderetreat as part of the Global Day of Coderetreat
Day-of Instructions / Guidelines Instructions and guidelines for what to do on the big day
Venue Preparation Checklist A list of items to check when preparing a venue for a coderetreat
Flyer Template A Google Drawings template you can use to create a flyer to help promote your event
GDCR host calendar (all times in UTC) Important events and milestones leading up to the Global Day of Coderetreat. Add the calendar to your Google Calendar to see all event dates and times in your local timezone.
Global sponsor prospectus Sponsor prospectus you can share with companies who might be interested in sponsoring GDCR
GDCR 2016 Google Drive Folder Contains documents with information and guidance on hosting a coderetreat as part of GDCR 2016
coderetreat.org Contains tips, blogs, photos, and other useful information about Coderetreat
GDCR website Provides general information about GDCR and information for global sponsors.

Learn

The following resources will help you learn how to host or facilitate a coderetreat:
Host training sessions Organizing/hosting a coderetreat for the first time? This training is for you! Host training covers the role of the host, host responsibilities, and tips and tricks for having an excellent day.
Facilitator training sessions Facilitating a coderetreat? You are invited to attend one of these training sessions. Facilitator training covers the role of the facilitator, facilitation techniques, choosing exercises, and other facilitation tips and tricks.
Recorded facilitator training (2012) A recording of a full facilitator training session from 2012. Watching this video is a good alternative if you are unable to attend a live training session.
Recorded host training (2015) A short recording of a host training session from 2015. Watching this video is a good alternative if you are unable to attend a live training session.

Help the global coordinators

We are always looking for new volunteers to help us coordinate the global event. We are looking for volunteers to help with:

  • designing and managing the websites
  • training new hosts and facilitators
  • creating promotional materials and templates for use by local event hosts
  • helping with general event record-keeping and tracking

If you would like to take-it-up-a-notch by helping with any of the above, or if you know someone who might be able to help, please email us! Together, we can make this event more amazing!
