Global Day Of Coderetreat is a community event happening on November 6 - November 7 2020. Join this year and practice your skills, embrace new paradigms and get to meet new people from all over the world!

With COVID-19 having a severe impact on the availability of on-site events, we're embracing the virtual events we had in the past more than ever. We encourage you to stay safe. If an on-site event isn't for you, we hope you'll find a remote event that matches your timezone.

If you're considering organizing your own event, we're here to support you on the journey of organizing your first coderetreat. Head over to the Facilitators Section for Training events, checklists, facilitation videos and more!
